Vietnam

For the adventurous travellers and the food lovers, Vietnam has it all. The land is vast, ranging from vibrant beaches to tropical rainforests. It is a seamless fusion of beauty. The country has a rich culture and history, along with its French-colonial influence. In its cosmopolitan cities, every corner you turn will be teeming with people, architecture, and food. Exploring Vietnam is a journey one cannot miss.

Special Homage

This site is dedicated to Vietnamese cuisine, but it would bother me if I didn't mention Phú Quốc. It's the largest island of Vietnam located off the south-western coast. I went there with my family, and the memories I've made during that time have become my most memorable. We stayed at luxury hotel resort that was located along a beach; and it wasn't any ordinary beach—the sand was white and the shore was sparkling and crystal clear! A complete opposite of Houston. The tiny fish that crept toward me while I was swimming made my experience all the better. If you love marine life, then this is perfect place for you.
